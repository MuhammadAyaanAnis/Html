SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 11 focuses on making cities and communities inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. This goal recognizes the rapid urbanization of the world and the challenges it brings, such as overcrowding, pollution, lack of housing, and strained infrastructure.
